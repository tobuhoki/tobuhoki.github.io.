We are a design collective based in Berlin and Halle, dedicated to crafting meaningful and aesthetically engaging work. Our focus lies in creating books, catalogues, and visual identities tailored specifically for the cultural sector, with a strong emphasis on collaboration and storytelling.
We strive to merge form and function, ensuring that each project reflects the unique voice and mission of our clients.
Selected clients include:
Staatliche Kunstsammungen Dresden, Haus der Kulturen der Welt, Künstlerhaus Bethanien Berlin, Stiftung Museum Schloss Moyland, Distanz Verlag, Kehrer Verlag, DCV, Walther & Franz König, KFW Stiftung
